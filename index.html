About

A Thing A Week is a community of developers focused on creating awesome products weekly. We are an inclusive community where people can increase their development skills. We want to make an awesome community where people can learn to develop and code better!


How It Works

At the beginning of the week on Sunday night, a message is sent out to the entire week on what the theme for the week is. From that point, you can start to build your awesome product! You have until Friday night to finish the code, and then submit it!
